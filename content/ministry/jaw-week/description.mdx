Jesus Awareness Week (JAW) is a 3-day event hosted by multiple UTSC Christian Clubs. We will be hosting daytime and evening events to meet UTSC students, share our faith stories, discuss the big questions about life and faith, and praise our God on campus.

JAW will be held from **Nov. 12-14, 2024**. Come meet us in the BV Hallway between **11am-2pm!**
